# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This project focuses on developing an **HR Analytics Dashboard** using Power BI to analyze employee attrition and uncover key factors affecting workforce retention. The dashboard provides actionable insights into employee demographics, experience levels, salary distribution, job roles, and workplace satisfaction, enabling organizations to make data-driven HR decisions.

---

## Problem Statement

Employee attrition can significantly impact organizational productivity, recruitment costs, and overall business performance. This project aims to identify the primary drivers of employee turnover, including:

* Work experience
* Salary levels
* Department-wise attrition
* Job satisfaction
* Work environment factors

The insights generated help organizations implement targeted strategies to improve employee satisfaction, engagement, and retention.

## Methodology

### 1. Data Preparation

* Imported raw HR data into Power BI using Power Query.
* Performed data cleaning, validation, and transformation to ensure data accuracy.

### 2. Data Integration

* Loaded the cleaned dataset into Power BI for analysis and reporting.

### 3. Data Modeling

* Created calculated columns and measures using DAX.
* Developed relationships and KPIs for attrition analysis.

### 4. Dashboard Development

* Designed an interactive dashboard featuring:

  * KPI Cards
  * Slicers
  * Attrition Analysis Charts
  * Department-wise Reports
  * Employee Demographics Visualizations

## Key Insights

### 📉 Experience-Based Attrition

* Employees with **less than 5 years of experience** account for **68% of total attrition**.

### 💰 Salary Impact

* Approximately **68% of employees leaving the organization earn below $5,000**, indicating salary as a major contributing factor.

### 🏢 Department Analysis

* The **Research & Development (R&D)** department experiences the highest attrition rate.

### 🎓 Fresher Retention Challenges

* A significant number of employees leaving the R&D department are freshers, suggesting issues related to onboarding, career development, or job expectations.

### 😊 Workplace Satisfaction

* Nearly **49% of employees report dissatisfaction with their work environment**, highlighting a critical area for improvement.

---

## Recommendations

### 1. Improve Compensation Strategy

* Review and increase salaries for employees earning below $5,000 to remain competitive in the market.

### 2. Strengthen Career Development

* Introduce structured mentorship programs and clear career progression paths for early-career employees.

### 3. Enhance Workplace Flexibility

* Offer remote and hybrid work options to improve employee satisfaction and work-life balance.

### 4. Increase Employee Engagement

* Conduct regular employee engagement surveys and take proactive action on feedback.

### 5. Focus on R&D Retention

* Implement recognition and reward programs within the R&D department to improve morale and reduce turnover.
